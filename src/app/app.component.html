FAQ

When will the cloud city will launch?

We have a very detailed roadmap that lays out our plan for the next 5 years. The alpha launch is set for Q2 of 2022.

Will The Cloud City tokens support staking?

The platform will allow staking of both its DAO token (ASTRAL) and NFTs so holders can earn a passive income through staking.

What are Business Enclaves?

To achieve true Ready Player One experience we believe that citizen's should be able to make real life transactions on the metaverse. For that we have introduced a dedicated area for businesses to set up shop and be able to sell real world goods. We will integrate third party APIs of these businesses to make sure the experience is seamless.

How can i earn on The Cloud City Metaverse?

There are multiple income generation possibilities on The Cloud City Metaverse, the most common being through P2E games, others include; staking, trading NFTs, renting out land, setting up businesses and much more. We have a lot more planned for the future.

Will The Cloud City Metaverse be a VR experience?

As much as we want this to be a VR experience, we believe that VR is still not the get go hardware for an average user. We are going to launch on PC with a download-able launcher. Mobile versions and VR are on our roadmap at a later stage.

What blockchain will host the metaverse?

As of now the chosen blockchain is Solana, but our dev team is looking into DAO possibilities with BSC and Unreal Engine. If successful we will migrate to BSC.
